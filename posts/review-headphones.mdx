## Design and Comfort

The XM5s feature a sleek, modern design that is lighter and more comfortable than previous models. The soft leather fit is perfect for long listening sessions.

## Sound Quality

As expected from Sony, the sound quality is exceptional. The bass is punchy without being overwhelming, and the mids and highs are crystal clear.

## The Verdict

If you are looking for the best noise-canceling headphones money can buy, the Sony WH-1000XM5 is a top contender.
